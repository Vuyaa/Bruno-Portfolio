About Bruno

My name is Bruno Vujevic and I am a Full Stack web developer, born and raised in Croatia, city of Split.

My journey into the IT world began with the CareerFoundry program, which was a very valuable experience for me because that helped me, not only in a technical sense, but introduced me to a world unknown to me.

From my first day, I started working independently on interesting projects using the guidelines provided from the studying materials.

I realized that programming is hard but also very rewarding. Nothing gives me more pleasure than building something out of nothing, to be able to solve a problem, to "unstuck the mechanism", gives this process thrilling feel.

I used full stack (MERN/MEAN tech-stack package) in application development. I also used various other frameworks and libraries that expanded the stack I was using. For example typeScript, react-native, bootstrap, jQuery...
